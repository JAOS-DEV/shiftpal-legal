# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 16, 2025

**Effective Date:** December 16, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to ShiftPal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Password (encrypted and securely stored)
- Account creation date

### Work-Related Information

To provide our shift tracking and pay calculation services, we collect:

- Shift start and end times
- Break durations
- Work dates
- Pay rates and settings
- Tax and deduction information
- Shift notes and tags
- Calculated pay information

### Automatically Collected Information

- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Usage statistics and crash reports (if analytics enabled)
- Authentication tokens

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**

   - Track your work shifts
   - Calculate your pay
   - Store your work history
   - Sync data across your devices

2. **Improve Our Service**

   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features
   - Understand how users interact with the app

3. **Communication**

   - Send important service updates
   - Respond to your support requests
   - Notify you about your account (with your permission)

4. **Security**
   - Protect against unauthorized access
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Maintain data integrity

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services, which are hosted on secure servers. Firebase complies with major security standards including:

- SOC 2/SOC 3
- ISO 27001
- GDPR (for European users)
- CCPA (for California users)

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- End-to-end encryption for data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted password storage
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and user authentication

### Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while your account is active
- Deleted account data: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Backup data: May persist in backups for up to 90 days

- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your shift data with employers or third parties

### We MAY Share Data:

1. **With Your Consent**

   - When you explicitly authorize us to share information

2. **Service Providers**

   - Google Firebase (database and authentication)
   - Crash reporting services (e.g., Sentry) - if enabled
   - Analytics providers - if enabled

3. **Legal Requirements**

   - To comply with applicable laws and regulations
   - To respond to valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
   - To protect our rights and safety

4. **Business Transfers**
   - In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data

- View all your stored information within the app
- Request a copy of your data

### Modify Your Data

- Edit your shifts and settings at any time
- Update your account information

### Delete Your Data

- Delete individual shifts or data
- Delete your entire account from Settings > Account
- Request complete data deletion by contacting us

### Opt-Out Options

- Disable analytics and crash reporting
- Opt out of non-essential notifications
- Revoke app permissions (may limit functionality)

### For EU Users (GDPR)

- Right to data portability
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing

### For California Users (CCPA)

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information

- Right to non-discrimination
